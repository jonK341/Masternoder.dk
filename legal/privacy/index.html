Privacy Policy

MasterNoder.dk — last updated June 2026

1. Data we collect

  • Account identifiers (user ID, email when provided via OAuth)
  • Linked platform IDs (Discord, Facebook, Google) when you connect accounts
  • Gameplay, casino, hosting order, and payment metadata (not full card numbers — PayPal handles those)
  • Technical logs (IP, user agent) for security and abuse prevention

2. How we use data

To operate the service, process payments, grant Discord Linked Roles, deliver hosting, send optional notifications, and improve the platform. We do not sell personal data.

3. Discord & OAuth

When you link Discord we store your Discord user ID to credit play-earn rewards and verify Hosting VIP eligibility. Discord OAuth scopes are limited to identify, email, and role_connections.write for Linked Roles.

4. Retention & deletion

Logs and account data are retained while your account is active. You may request deletion via Profile settings or support. Some financial records may be kept as required by law.

5. Cookies & local storage

We use browser local storage for session preferences (e.g. game_user_id) and standard cookies for auth where applicable.

6. Your rights (EU/EEA)

You may access, correct, or delete personal data, and object to processing where applicable. Contact us via the site support channels.
