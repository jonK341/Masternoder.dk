# Sound Pack — Cinematic Stingers

Curated SFX list and licensing notes for video overlays on MasterNoder generator output.

## Included stingers (reference IDs)

| ID | Mood | Suggested use |
|----|------|----------------|
| cin-rise-01 | Rising tension | Documentary chapter open |
| cin-hit-02 | Impact | Scene transition |
| cin-whoosh-03 | Motion | Title card fly-in |
| cin-pad-04 | Ambient bed | End card hold |

## Mixing notes

- Peak normalize stingers to −3 dBFS before layering under voiceover.
- High-pass at 120 Hz on impact hits to avoid mud on mobile speakers.
- Crossfade 80–120 ms when chaining back-to-back stingers.
