How paying works

Everything in the catalog is priced in play-earned currency or optional top-ups. Pick the path that fits you — the game systems underneath stay the same.

Play & pointsEarn coins and unified points in-game, then spend them here on themes, boosters, and upgrades.
PayPalFiat on-ramp: coin packs and Pro subscription on the PayPal & coins tab — instant credits after checkout.
MN2Optional native token: deposit for an in-wallet balance, pay from balance in the catalog, or use Pay on-chain for a one-time address and exact amount. MN2 Services category links to masternode hosting ($4.99 PayPal), on-ramp, staking, and market.

Revenue map (for operators)

Aligns with MONETIZATION_PAYPAL.md §1–5: packs, tiers, subscriptions, B2B/SCR, and marketplace. Shop UI and Profile use the same purchase APIs; smoke checks: scripts/shop_v4_production_smoke.py.

  • 1 — Coin / credit packs: PayPal & coins → PayPal coin packs (/api/shop/coin-packs, /api/paypal/create-order) — primary fiat → coins path.
  • 2 — Premium tiers: server-enforced caps via unified points; surfaced on Profile and generator upsell.
  • 3 — Subscriptions: Pro on the same tab (/api/monetization/subscription/create → PayPal approve URL).
  • 4 — B2B / Studio Cash Rail: SKUs in data/monetization_config.json; manual settlement via monetization B2B API (ops).
  • 5 — Catalog checkout: /api/game/shop/purchase / /api/shop-v3/purchase — coins, unified points, in-wallet MN2, or on-chain MN2 order payment for coin-priced lines.
